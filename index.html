About Us

Driven by Excellence Since 1996

Founded by Vilas B. Kharat, a Government-Licensed Electrical Contractor & Consulting Engineer with experience since March 22, 1996, LAV-KUSH Electrical Engineering Works has become a trusted name in Mumbai’s electrical industry.

We specialize in safe, efficient, and reliable electrical installations, maintenance, automation, and liaisoning services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in the electrical industry, delivering safe, innovative, and sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional services with safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction at the core.
